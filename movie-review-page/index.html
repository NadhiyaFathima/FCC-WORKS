Inception (2010)

Inception is a mind-bending sci-fi thriller where professional thief Dom Cobb infiltrates dreams to steal secrets from deep within the subconscious. When tasked with performing the impossible — planting an idea (inception) — Cobb and his skilled team navigate shifting dreamscapes, battling both external enemies and the ghosts of his past.
