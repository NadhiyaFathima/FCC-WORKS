Cold coffee recipe!

Cold Coffee is the cozy-meets-cool drink of dreams-perfect for hot days, study sessions etc.Cold coffee is loved for its smooth, rich flavour without the bite of heat,and it pairs beautifully with ice, milk and a bit of sweetness.

Ingredients

  • Instant coffee(1 Tb)
  • Sugar(to taste)
  • Warm water(1/4 cup)
  • Cold milk(1 cup)
  • Ice cubes
  • One scoop vanilla ice-cream(optional)

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, mix the coffee, sugar and warm water until its fully dissolved and frothy.
  2. Add this mix into a blender along with the cold milk and a few ice cubes.
  3. Blend for about 30-40 seconds until its smooth and frothy.
  4. Pour into a tall glass, add more ice if you like, and if you're feeling extra-top it off with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a sprinkle of cocoa powder.
